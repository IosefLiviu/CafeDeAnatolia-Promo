## TIPS FOR AI VIDEO GENERATION

1. **Consistency:** Use the same style keywords across all prompts: "cinematic, warm amber, golden, slow motion, 4K"
2. **Upscale:** Generate at highest resolution, then upscale with Topaz Video AI if needed
3. **Duration:** Most AI tools generate 4-8s clips. Plan to stitch multiple clips together
4. **Color grade after:** Apply consistent color grading in post-production (DaVinci Resolve free) to unify all clips
5. **Music sync:** Generate clips slightly longer than needed, then trim to match music beats in editing
6. **Avoid faces:** AI-generated faces can look uncanny. Stick to silhouettes, hands, close-ups of objects, wide crowd shots
7. **Loop points:** For backgrounds (Script 02), generate with smooth start/end frames for seamless looping
