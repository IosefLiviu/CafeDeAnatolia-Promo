## INFLUENCER / PARTNERSHIP STRATEGY

### Micro-influencers (5K–50K followers)
- **Who:** Bucharest nightlife bloggers, music pages, lifestyle creators
- **Deal:** 2 free tickets + backstage mention in exchange for 1 Reel + 3 Stories
- **Target:** 5-10 micro-influencers
- **When:** Week 2-3

### Music pages / Communities
- **Who:** Romanian electronic music Facebook groups, IG pages like @bucharest.nightlife, local DJ communities
- **Deal:** Cross-promotion, event listing, shared posts
- **When:** Week 1 onwards

### Artist cross-promotion
- **Who:** Rialians On Earth, Billy Esteban, Keto van Harum, Jasmine — their own social channels
- **Ask:** Share the event poster + ticket link on their stories/feed
- **When:** Week 2 (lineup reveal)
