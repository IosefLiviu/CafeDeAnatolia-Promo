## PRODUCTION NOTES

- If no real footage available, use stock from Pexels/Artgrid: search "desert night", "smoke particles", "DJ silhouette", "crowd golden hour"
- VFX: After Effects — Trapcode Particular for sand/dust particles on logo reveal
- Color: Use LUT "Orange & Teal" variant, push warm tones, crush blacks
- Typography: Sans-serif, thin weight, wide letter-spacing (think: Montserrat Light, uppercase)
- Transitions: Cross-dissolve for slow sections, hard cuts for fast montage section
