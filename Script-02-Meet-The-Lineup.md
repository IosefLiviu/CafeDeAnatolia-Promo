## ANIMATION NOTES

### Artist Name Transitions (pick one style for consistency):
- **Option A — Glitch:** Name glitches in with digital distortion, settles clean
- **Option B — Reveal:** Gold line draws across screen, text revealed behind it
- **Option C — Kinetic type:** Letters fall/slide in one by one, synced to beat

### 4-to-1 Merge (Shot 6):
- Each artist panel occupies 1/4 of the screen
- All 4 visible for 1 second
- Then panels slide together, cross-dissolving into the venue shot
- Feels like "all artists converging on one location"
