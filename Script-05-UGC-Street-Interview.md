# SCRIPT 05 — "Street Interview / UGC" (Organic/Relatable)

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Format:** 9:16 (Vertical — TikTok priority, then Reels)
**Mood:** Casual, authentic, unscripted feel. "Caught on the street." Not polished — that's the point.
**Color Grade:** Minimal. Natural daylight. Maybe slight warmth boost. Should feel like a phone recording.

## CASTING / PEOPLE NOTES

You need 4-5 people. They can be:

| Option | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Real street interviews** | Actually go out with a phone + mic and ask people. Best authenticity. |
| **Friends/influencers** | Brief them loosely, let them improvise. Don't over-script. |
| **Micro-influencers** | 5K-50K followers in the music/nightlife/lifestyle niche in Bucharest. Trade a free ticket for a 10-second reaction. |

**Key rules:**
- Diverse group (age, style, gender)
- Must feel REAL, not acted. Imperfect = better.
- Small reactions matter: eye widening, looking at friend, pulling out phone
- If someone says something unscripted and great, USE IT

## DIALOGUE OPTIONS (Romanian)

The above dialogue is a guide. Here are alternative lines if people improvise differently:

**Q: "Ai auzit de Cafe De Anatolia?"**
- "Cine n-a auzit? Sunt peste tot pe Instagram."
- "Da, am ascultat mixurile lor... e altfel de muzica."
- "Stiu, stiu! Cu muzica aia organica, nu?"

**Q: "Vin in Bucuresti pe 25 aprilie."**
- "Nu cred! In sfarsit!"
- "Serios? Unde? Trebuie sa fiu acolo."
- "De cand asteptam asa ceva in Bucuresti!"
- "Gata, nu mai am nicio scuza."

**Closer:**
- "Iau bilet acum, nu risc."
- "Daca nu esti acolo, nu existi."
- "Ma duc, clar."

## TECHNICAL SPECS

| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| Camera | Phone is fine (iPhone 14+ / Samsung S23+). That's the point. |
| Stabilization | Handheld, slight movement OK. Do NOT use gimbal — too smooth kills the UGC feel |
| Microphone | Clip-on wireless mic on interviewer OR directional shotgun mic on phone. Audio clarity matters even if video is casual |
| Lighting | Natural daylight. Shoot during golden hour (17:00-19:00) for best look |
| Location | High-traffic Bucharest spots: Centrul Vechi, Calea Victoriei, Herastrau, Piata Universitatii |
| Framing | Close to medium shot. Person fills 60-70% of frame. Background visible but blurred. |

---

## EDITING NOTES

- Jump cuts between people (no transitions — hard cuts only)
- Subtle zoom-in on reaction moments (post-production, 105-110% scale)
- Captions: white text with black outline or background box, TikTok-style placement (center-lower third)
- The transition from interviews to event poster (Shot 6) should feel like a "record scratch" moment — energy shift
- Add subtle screen shake on the logo reveal
- End card: keep it 3 seconds, clean, readable on small screen

---

## CAPTIONS STYLE (for on-screen text)

```
Font: Bold, rounded sans-serif (like TikTok's default or Poppins Bold)
Size: Large enough to read at arm's length on phone
Position: Below speaker's face, above bottom nav bar
Color: White with black stroke or dark semi-transparent background
Animation: Pop-in word by word (synced to speech), not all at once
```

## POSTING INSTRUCTIONS

- **TikTok:** Post between 18:00-21:00 on a weekday. Pin comment with ticket link.

- **Stories:** Cut into 3 clips (interviews / reveal / CTA) for story sequence.
